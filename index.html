Why Listen to me?

Okay, maybe I’m not really a book expert, but I have been an avid reader for a while now. Not to brag, but I got my first library card in first grade, and completed all the summer reading challenges they hosted. I’m no refined reader by any means the only classics I’ve really read were assigned readings in high school. But I think that makes me the perfect person to get your reviews from. Sure, classics are great pieces of work, but sometimes you want to read about zombies vs aliens, or dragon tamers, or trashy romance novels. And that's where my expertise lies
